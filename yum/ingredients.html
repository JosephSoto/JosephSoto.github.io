TAGINE OF CHICKEN
With Preserved Lemon & Olives

  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 onions, grated or very finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp crushed saffron threads or saffron powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 chicken, cut up in 6 or 8 pieces
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  • 2 tbsp chopped flat leaf parsley
  • 12 green or violet olives
  • peel from large preserved lemon
  • 1/2 juice of squeezed lemon
  • salt
  • black pepper
