Lemon Recipe Cards

Given a packet of lemon-themed recipes, we were asked to design ten recipe cards and complementary packaging. I explored stamping and scanning lemons as a means of expressing the lemon-themed project. Assignment for Lynn Faitelson's Typography II course Student work for Massachusetts College of Art & Design.
